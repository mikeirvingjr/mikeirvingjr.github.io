Gunnar

We will describe the experience coding this game.

Description

Gunnar is a about a young soldier breaking the rules to save his father whom has been discovered to go missing. He teams up with a few of his friends to search for clues and get to the bottom of his father's dissapearance.

Influences

  • Metal Slug™
  • Contra™
  • Gears of War™

Genre

  • Run and gun

Developers

  • Productive Sloth Entertainment (Me)

Publishers

Main Characters

Gregg Gunnar Gundersen

Edgar

Retired now, but once served as Gunnar's training officer

Kelso

All around crazy; you have one in every group

Dimitri

Explosives expert. He goes so far as to not even use a gun

Simon

Peter

Features

  • Customizable characters
    • Ability to change skin tone
    • Ability to change clothing
  • 2-player CO-OP
  • Elemental grenades and bullets
    • Fire
    • Water/Ice
    • Acid
    • Electricity
